Install Room Refresh

Buying furniture online is high friction when shoppers cannot picture scale, placement, or style. Room Refresh puts a room-visualization button directly on product pages with no theme edits required, then helps merchants track usage and related orders from the app dashboard.

How it works

One room photo, one product photo,
one clear buying decision.

  1. Upload a room photo. Shoppers start with their own living room, bedroom, office, or entryway.
  2. Select the product. A sofa, lamp, mirror, wall clock, or accent piece is pulled from the product page.
  3. Generate the preview. Room Refresh returns an in-space visualization that makes size and style easier to judge.
