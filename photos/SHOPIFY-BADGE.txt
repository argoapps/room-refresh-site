Shopify App Store badge
----------------------
Download the official badge from:
https://shopify.dev/zip/shopify-app-store-badges.zip

Extract the zip and save the WHITE-BACKGROUND version (black logo + "Find it on the Shopify App Store") as:
  shopify-app-store-badge.png
in this folder.

The header button uses this image and links to your app. Do not resize, crop, or alter the badge (see Shopify's guidelines).
